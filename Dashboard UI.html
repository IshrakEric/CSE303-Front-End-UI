Comprehensive Heart Health Monitoring System

A Comprehensive Heart Health Monitoring System is an integrated platform designed to track and analyze cardiovascular health in real-time. It utilizes wearable devices to monitor vital signs such as heart rate, blood pressure, and ECG. Data is transmitted to a centralized application that employs AI to detect anomalies, provide insights, and alert healthcare professionals of potential issues. This system enhances early diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and continuous health management, aiming to reduce the risk of heart disease and improve patient outcomes through proactive and preventive care.
